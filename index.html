About Us

MTG is an exclusive distributor of premium quality food products, operating in the MENA and South East Asia. We are proud of our strict selection of products that we represent and promote in the international arena. We offer only the most recognized bestsellers that won recognition for their outstanding taste and quality.

Products

Black Angus Beef

The secret of real meat is the correctly selected beef breed of Aberdeen Angus steers, fattening with grain grown in farm fields, processing at the latest automated enterprise and quality control at all stages of production. Marbled beef is meat with high fat concentration.

Marbled beef steaks are the standard of rich taste, juiciness and tenderness of meat dishes. The character of the steak largely depends on the cut (part of the carcass) from which it is made. Middle cuts are known all over the world for the simplicity of cooking and guaranteed excellent results, secondary cuts are popular among experienced cooks for a rich range of flavors and a variety of serving.

We distribute a full range of premium and secondary cuts and steaks from marbled beef.

The Black Angus advantages

100 % quality, naturalness and the unequalled taste are the main advantages of all the Black Angus products line. Cattle grows without the usage of antibiotics and growth hormones. All steers gain weight under comfortable and humane farming conditions- firstly on the farms and pastures, then in the specialized fattening facilities. Minimum contact with humans help the cattle grow without stress, which improves qualities of meat.

Wagyu Beef

Long-term fattening according to the Slow Grow system, completely stress-free content and the best genetics allow our suppliers to create meat of category B3. No wonder Wagyu beef is considered a delicacy along with white truffle, foie gras and Swiss cheeses.

The Wagyu Beef advantages

Wagyu is a world–famous delicacy that, along with sushi and sake, has become a symbol of the Land of the Rising Sun. There are legends about the cultivation of steers of this breed: as if on Japanese farms they are soldered off with beer and sake, massaged daily and hung on belts so that the animal moves as little as possible. However, in fact, to get real Wagyu beef, you only need the best genetics, the right nutrition system and completely stress-free content. Bulls are fattened according to the Slow Grow system for 500 days on a diet of corn, silage and pure spring water. This allows them to gain weight gradually, so fat is formed inside the muscles and creates marbling.

Mix Breed

This is the selected meat of young bulls grown on the best farms in the countryside. They eat only natural food and receive special care and attention. This is how farmers we work with create a delicious and healthy product that meets the highest quality standards.

The Mix Breed advantages

Free grazing of animals is one of the main conditions for their maintenance, thanks to which farmers get healthy cattle. The diet of animals includes both herbs and hay, as well as cereals. Thanks to such a combined diet, bulls grow up healthy, with strong immunity and developed muscle mass. The natural conditions in which the bulls live, minimal interference in their lives by people and careful care affect the quality of the finished product. Observing fairly simple principles of keeping and raising livestock, manufacturers get delicious and tender meat.

Lamb Dorper

Only the meat of young lambs no older than 4 months can be called young lamb. It is an exclusively dietary product rich in proteins, essential amino acids and vitamins. Dorper lambs are highly regarded as a prime lamb producing breed as they are fast maturing with the ability to produce a high-quality carcass with excellent conformation and fat distribution. Our manufacturers raise their lambs on a modern eco-farms, where farm-workers closely monitor their diet and health. Everything is under strict control: from natural feeds to stress-free animal rearing technologies. This is a guarantee of excellent quality lamb on your table.

Rose Veal

Pink veal is a dietary meat of calves under the age of 8 months. It has a delicate structure and a mild taste that goes well with different side dishes and sauces. It is from veal that the main delicacies are prepared in all cuisines of the world: from Georgian chakapuli to Italian tagliata or veal blanket, which are so appreciated by French gourmets. The meat of calves is low in fat and has a lot of useful trace elements and protein, which is easily digested.

Pork

Our manufacturers have been a permanent leader in this segment since 2010. And because of that our pork meets all modern quality standards. We also distribute Tender Pork which is our new unique product. Pig meat undergoes a wet aging procedure — then it is packed in vacuum bags and store it in special rooms for three weeks. This is the optimal period for which pork acquires a rich and unusual taste, becomes especially soft and tender. Now everyone can cook an exquisite meat delicacy in their own kitchen without much effort!

Sausage Products

Sausage products we distribute are made from excellent meat from farms of our manufacturers. Unique cooking technologies, knowledge of traditions and good taste – that’s what our sausages is. We care about those who buy our products, so we never compromise on quality issues. In our catalog we have sausages with milk, eggs, spicy nutmeg. And all of this with tasty natural spices!

Chicken Nuggets

Chicken nuggets are the most tender pieces of poultry meat in golden breading. At the same time crispy and juicy, they will appeal to even the most sophisticated gourmet. Chicken snacks in crispy breaded wheat crackers are a great option for a quick snack. Combine hearty slices with classic ketchup, soft cheese or bright sweet and sour sauce. Create your own flavor combinations, complement your favorite dishes and surprise your friends with our crumpets. The entire range is presented in our catalog.

Chicken

We offer you the most delicious and healthy poultry products, all of them were produced from the best quality chicken, grown on the balanced natural feed. In terms of protein, poultry meat is not inferior to beef and lean pork, while the fat content in it is significantly less, as well as cholesterol harmful to health. Expand your daily menu with a dozen new dishes from delicious chicken - from BBQ wings and grilled sausages, baked juicy chicken breasts, Asian style to curry chicken!
